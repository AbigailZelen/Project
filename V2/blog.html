Overlays

Overlays were introduced to me by a photographer in an art gallery. Since then, I’ve enjoyed experimenting with Overlays and different techniques. Overlays occur when you layer two images and make one of them opaque. Doing this gives the images a unique look and causes you to look at the images longer until your eye can figure out what’s actually happening.
