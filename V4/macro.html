Macro:

Macro is one of my very favorite styles of photography. It gives you a chance to take a closer look at something and notice details.

Macro Stacks:

Macro Stacks are put together using a series of images. These images are composed of around 20 images each. Each image has something different in focus giving you a fully focused image. It also creates a greater depth of field as the subject is very focused and the background is fully out of focus.
